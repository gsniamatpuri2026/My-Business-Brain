## SECTION 3: HOW DELIVERY WORKS

1. Initial conversation — Gurmel assesses the client's actual situation: where they are, what they're trying to protect or build, what they want, and whether the programme or pathway under consideration makes sense for their specific profile.
2. Programme recommendation — If appropriate, Gurmel presents the real options, with genuine timelines, actual costs, and risks that are not obscured or omitted.
3. Engagement and document preparation — Once the client is ready to proceed, Gurmel manages the full documentation chain: source-of-funds structuring, legalisation, translations, due diligence, notarisation.
4. Application submission and case management — Gurmel prepares and submits the application, manages follow-up, and handles any queries from the relevant authority.
5. Approval and post-approval guidance — On approval, the client receives a complete, documented file. Gurmel provides clarity on what follows: renewals, travel rights, tax considerations.

Throughout: the client deals with Gurmel directly. No handoffs. No junior intermediaries. Every stage is explained before it happens.

## SECTION 9: COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

**Tone by context:**
- LinkedIn posts: Direct, substantive, industry-honest — the voice of someone who has seen the inside of this industry and is not impressed by its standard practices. No cheerleading. No generic inspiration. Specific, grounded, occasionally blunt.
- Sales conversations: Consultative, unhurried. Honest about fit before promoting the service. The goal is clarity for the client, not a closed deal on a programme that doesn't suit them.
- Client onboarding: Professional and structured. Every stage explained before it happens. The client should never have to ask "what happens next?"

**Language to use:**
clarity · honest picture · source of funds · case officer · profile · prepared · options · file · residency · citizenship · programme · permanent · legalisation · stakes · transparent · institutional knowledge

**Language to avoid:**
guaranteed · seamless · hassle-free · fast-track · industry-leading · passionate about immigration · tailored solutions · one-stop shop · success rate · stress-free

**Hard limits:**
Never quote approval rates that cannot be substantiated. Never present a programme as suitable without a genuine profile assessment. Never delegate the client relationship to a junior without the client's explicit understanding.
