## SECTION 1: ORIGIN & FOUNDATION

### The Moment That Changed Everything
I was working in the Hong Kong Immigration Department, but my job finished in September 2019. Before that, I had already started a property purchase deal based on my job, but suddenly my job finished and I had to pay for the property deal. The mortgage approval was required, and the property was completed five months ahead of schedule, so we had to pay for it right when my job had just ended. Also, this was the time when Covid already started, so the situation was very tough. It was difficult to arrange the money to complete the property deal. I tried various methods, like seeking help from friends and relatives, but it was not helpful. I lost my job, and there was no work. I had part-time work, but everything stopped, and there was no source of income.

This was 2019 when Covid-19 started, and later on the situation became worse and worse. This was also when I thought I needed to plan how to work and manage my life, because I had two daughters. It was my responsibility to take care of them, my family, and my parents. I thought about how I should work, so this was the situation when I thought about my work to be more successful in the future and avoid situations like this in the future.

It was very difficult to get the property deal done, but with the help of my trust in God, with the blessing of God, finally we had communication with the developer to give some exemption. Finally, the deal was successfully completed, but it was 99% difficult, because at that time in the same property development a lot of buyers had to leave their deal because of financial problems. I am fortunate that the deal was completed.

### The Person Who Shaped Them
Guru Nanak is my divine mentor who taught me the three principles: Girth Karo, Naam Japo, Vatsyako — which means earn with fair means, meditation of God's name, and share with others. Guru Nanak's teachings still affect my words and my actions today. Guru Nanak taught me to do work hard and earn with fair means, be truthful, and do the meditation of God's name to make my life spiritually healthy.

Guru Nanak also taught me to help the needy people, help the society and the people by sharing whatever you have with the needy people. Guru Nanak not only teaches about religion and spirituality, but it also teaches that I should not remain idle. I should not be dependent on anybody. I should earn my livelihood by myself to take care of my family. I should not beg. Guru Nanak taught the whole life, not only about spirituality and religion. Guru Nanak does not stop me from being wealthy or being rich as long as I am following the religious teachings, I am helping the needy and the society, and I am practicing religious and spiritual things.

### The Value Running Their Decisions
Being honest and truthful is the principle which I think I am following, and I think it is a part of my life. It is still running my decision today. I want to be fair to everyone. I want to not do any injustice to anybody.

For example, in May 2026, I had a client who wanted to apply for a visa. After going through his profile, I honestly advised him about his profile. He was ready to pay a big amount of money for the visa, but we saw some limitations, some weaknesses in his case, which were making his case impossible. That's why, instead of taking the huge amount of money, we advised him. I showed him the true picture of his case, and I clearly told him that, with this profile and these kind of situations, his visa is impossible. However, even if we start now, because of these two limitations, his visa cannot be approved because it is not fulfilling the major criteria of the visa application. Why I did so — because I didn't want to let my client suffer without any possibility of a successful result. Doing business just for money or without creating any positive impact to the client is not my aim.

## SECTION 2: CHARACTER UNDER PRESSURE

### The Hardest Chapter
Starting from 2019, during the COVID time, 2019-20-21-22 was the most difficult time for me. In the beginning, I also lost my job, and I could not work as a part-time translator because most of the work had already stopped due to COVID-19. I had to remain at home like other people, with no job, no money, and it was very difficult to run the expenses. I want to become a successful businessman, having enough money in my bank account to keep my family happy and to help the needy people if anybody is coming to take my help.

During COVID, when I didn't have a job and didn't have money, I was mentally, socially, and financially broken from every aspect. It was looking like there was no meaning in my life. It was looking like my life had stopped, and this was the first time I felt such a difficult situation for such a long time in my life.

### The Risk That Defined Them
I took many risks in my life, like when I bought my first property in 2017. I didn't have enough money, but I had already decided that I wanted to get my property instead of being on rent. I had some money, but some of the money I borrowed, and later I returned it.

When I finally got my property, it was a good decision, because I was able to earn money and return money to my friends. Then I saved money by paying the rent, and with my own property I was just paying the mortgage installment, and it was a good decision. There was a risk under this decision that I would not be able to pay the installment, or that I would not be able to return the money which I borrowed from my friends. But ultimately, even through the COVID-19, I was still able to pay the installments, and I was still protected. I didn't face so much of a difficult situation where I had to leave my property, or where my installments were stopped.

### The Mistake That Built Them
I think I would like to tell my mistake, which caused me a loss. I started doing online trading of the share market or futures. Initially, I didn't want to do it, but with the influence of my company where I was working, the people were doing it. I first stopped myself from doing it, but later on I was influenced and I started doing it, and I didn't think about getting some training or some knowledge, but I just started it. I suffered a huge loss in this. I have been doing it again and again to recover my loss, but finally I lost a lot of money, which affected my life and my business also, because the money which I could invest in my business to have more profit and so was wasted in the share market loss.

### Standing Their Ground
During my business as an immigration consultant, I got a Saudi Arabia work visa case to be handled from Hong Kong, and the client was in China. It was something new, but I was thinking we could do it. It was very difficult, and there were many obstacles, like the requirements and the process not being the same as in Hong Kong. We took that case and put in all our efforts, and finally this case was successfully approved.

The client was very happy, and we took this risk because if we could do it the first time without any problem and despite so many difficulties, then we will get the mastery of this. The next cases we can do easily, and we can take more similar cases in the future with similar clients, which would be good for us. That's why I took this decision to do it.

## SECTION 3: THE WORK & THE WHY

### When They Knew
Before answering this question, I want to tell you about my education.

I did my bachelor's degree in commerce. After my 10th class in India, I wanted to pursue further education in science, but because of being from a poor family, the college was not in our village, so I would have had to travel to the city, and science also needed extra tuition classes, which I would have had to pay extra money for. Because of financial problems, I finally decided to take a commerce subject instead of science. I was more interested in science, however. No problem — I completed my BCom, and later I did a Post-Graduation Diploma in Computer Applications, the IT computer line. After that, I did a Master of Arts in Religion, specifically in Sikhism. That job required accounting knowledge, knowledge of religion, knowledge of computers, and everything that I had learned. That was the moment when I thought that what I had done in the past was being utilized now, and then I was very happy. I thought you should put all your efforts into what you are doing, and your destiny will keep you on the right track. You should do everything honestly.

### What They Do Differently
And now we are at my space. What I do from scratch, I do everything properly to make it smoother in the long run. The foundation must be strong. If we have a weak foundation, it will affect the life of anything and the future of anything. You should give much time in the beginning so that you can have a better foundation.

Because when I study the case from the beginning carefully and thoroughly and I have all the understanding of the various perspectives of the case, then we can plan it accordingly to make it successful. If we know it, then we can try to give a justifiable explanation of the refusal, which will not affect the current visa application in a negative way. This will be our strategy.

### The Reframe That Changes Everything
And then the idea, the perspective which I share is: whatever you do, you should do it in the right way, which has better results and is long-lasting. That's why whatever I do, people like it, so that's why people say what I do is good and why it is good, because I try to do it in a proper way to be more successful.

Suppose if we have to plan a program, then we will think: how to start, how to plan, what will be the problems, what is our target, what are the ways to reach our target. So we think about different such things, and then we choose the right way to do it.

### The Work That Made Them Proud
I had a female client who got married. Her previous husband had passed away, and she got married for the second time. When she applied for her second husband's visa, her visa was refused, and she was very much disappointed and very much upset.

They then came to know about us, about me, and told me about the situation. We went through her case and prepared it again. In one go, we submitted all the documents, letters, explanations, and everything. Fortunately, her husband's visa was approved without any difficulty and smoothly, and she was very happy that her husband's visa was approved. She could reunite with her husband in Hong Kong, and it was very helpful for them. It was a very happy moment for them, and it was a great situation for us when we feel proud of ourselves that we made somebody's life happy. And now her husband is working in Hong Kong and earning a good amount of money and supporting the family, supporting the wife. We feel that we have done something which is making somebody's life better. Even they have also told other people regarding our services, and they have spread the word of mouth that the visa was rejected and it was approved to GSN Consultancy, Mr. Gurmel Singh.

After taking our services, they are very happy and they think that we have provided them great quality of service, which had made their life happy and successful.
